Welcome to Ristorante Amalfi!

Located in the heart of Manchester, Ristorante Amalfi brings the essence of Italy to the city with its traditional yet contemporary Italian dining experience. The restaurant offers a warm, inviting atmosphere, perfect for a casual lunch, romantic dinner, or family gathering.

Featuring a menu full of classic Italian dishes, Ristorante Amalfi prides itself on using the freshest, locally sourced ingredients, alongside imports from Italy to maintain authentic flavors.
